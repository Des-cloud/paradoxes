Zeno's Paradox

Introduction

This is my favorite paradox of all time. Trying to reason this paradox out without rigorous mathematical proofs can make your brain squirm. It just seems so plausible logically even though you know it isn't in reality.
This paradox is attributed to Zeno of Elea (c. 450 BCE). This paradox is or was famously called the Paradox of the Tortoise and Achilles. The paradox can be rephrased simply as:

The Problem
